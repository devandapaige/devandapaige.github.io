Clementine was adopted by her forever-family in early 2017. It took her a while to warm up to her Mom, but the love she developed with her Dad was instant. Once she felt comfortable with her new home and family, she started putting on a bit of weight and started looking more like a cat, and less like a kitten. Her young-at-heart spirit stayed, and she taught herself how to play fetch with small foam balls.

Her favorite color is pink, and favorite thing to do is lie outside the warm sidewalk. She considers her Mom's home-office her bedroom. When both of her parents are home, she will always play favorites and choose to cuddle with her Dad. Unlike some cats, she likes to be held, get kisses, and prefers to be the center of attention whenever possible, which can sometimes hard when her sister, Kiwi, is around.
